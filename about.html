About ATMC

THE FIRST PRIVATE HOSPITAL OF GANDERBAL DISTRICT

ATMC is the first private hospital in Ganderbal district, strategically located at the crossroads between Srinagar city and the heart of Ganderbal.

Founded with a vision to serve the people of Ganderbal, ATMC is dedicated to addressing the healthcare needs of the district and ensuring accessible, high-quality medical care for all.

Staffed by a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals, our mission is to provide compassionate, patient-centered care across all specialties—ensuring that every individual receives the highest standard of treatment and support, right here in their own district.
