OUR COMMITMENT

ATMC is committed to safe, compliant, and environmentally responsible biomedical waste management. All procedures are designed to protect patients, healthcare workers, and the community while ensuring adherence to regulatory standards.

ATMC Waste Segregation Categories

White Category

Sharps including needles, syringes, scalpels, and blades requiring sterilization.

Red Category

Contaminated recyclable waste such as IV tubes, catheters, gloves, and plastics.

Blue Category

Glass waste including vials, ampoules, and broken contaminated glass items.

Yellow Category

Infectious waste including tissues, blood bags, dressings, and expired medicines.

Black Category

General non-hazardous waste such as paper, food waste, and packaging materials.

ATMC Biomedical Waste Management Flow

Segregation

Waste is separated at the source into color-coded categories.

Collection

Waste is collected in sealed containers by trained staff.

Transportation

Safe internal transport using dedicated routes.

Treatment

Waste is treated via autoclaving, incineration, or shredding.

Disposal

Final disposal through authorized biomedical waste facilities.
